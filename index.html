TEAM SOLO MID

My Favorite LCS LOLEsports Team

Team Solo Mid or for short TSM has been my favorite team since I started League of Legends. I have no idea why, but their mindset draws me in. TSM to me is always this very funny team that has amazing teamwork, through losses and wins im supporting them forever. Bjergsen and Doublelift are the two players I really admire, both of them are charasmatic, both are really good at their roles, both of them are calm and collective when in teamfights. I believe that one day TSM wuold win the World Championships. I know a lot of people would disagree with me since TSM is an NA team, and NA teams are usually not the best Teams in the League of Legends scene. But as a player coming from the NA Server, wouldn't it be incredible to wipe the smiles of all faces of the haters. Wouldn't it be nice to watch a team from NA beat the Koreans, the Chinese and the EU teams?
